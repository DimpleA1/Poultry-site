Weather flexibility

With the unpredictable nature of weather patterns, poultry farmers must employ weather-flexible strategies such as ventilation, heating, and cooling systems to maintain optimal conditions for their birds year-round.

Residue Treatment

It involves the proper management of waste materials produced by poultry farms, such as manure and bedding materials. This can include composting, anaerobic digestion, or other methods to reduce the environmental impact and potential health risks associated with these residues.

Exhaust Air

It refers to the air that is removed from poultry housing facilities through ventilation systems. This air contains dust, moisture, and potentially harmful gases, and proper management is essential to maintain air quality and prevent negative impacts on both bird health and human health..

Poultry Climate

Maintaining appropriate climate conditions is critical to ensure bird health, productivity, and welfare, as well as to prevent the spread of diseases. Climate control systems, such as heaters, fans, and cooling systems, are commonly used in poultry farming to achieve optimal climate conditions.

Eggs

eggs are the most commonly consumed type of egg worldwide, and are a staple in many diets due to their high protein content and versatility in cooking. These eggs come in a range of sizes and colors, from small to jumbo and white to brown, but their nutritional value remains relatively consistent. .
